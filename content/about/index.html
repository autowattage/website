Bunnyguy is a 17-year old self-proclaimed 'tinkering artist' and programmer based in the United States. They are most known for their TV-head cosplay twoswap and as an artist/merch designer for Hack Club.

Bunnyguy has previously contributed art to make stickers, postcards, jackets, PCBs, and more for Hack Club programs such as Counterspell, Summer of Making, Apocalpyse, and High Seas. In the summer of 2025, Bunnyguy became an intern to help organize Highway and Undercity.

In its free time, Bunnyguy likes to draw art, code websites, and play puzzle games.

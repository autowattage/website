Professional jargon

Modified about-me from the Hack Club team page:

Bunnyguy is a 17-year-old Viet-American artist and programmer based in the United States.
Bunnyguy has previously contributed art for Hack Club programs such as Apocalypse, Counterspell, High Seas, and Summer of Making.

Back in the summer of 2025, I interned at Hack Club to help organize Undercity, a 4-day hardware hackathon hosted at GitHub HQ. I made a bunch of art assets, including but not limited to:

  • Water bottles
  • Stickers
  • Postcards
  • Patches
  • PCB (art)

Krita is my go-to drawing program, but I have experience using Adobe Animate, Blender, Figma, and 3DS Max.

Cooler about-me

Despite being Vietnamese, I have little fluency; English is my preferred language. For those who are into personality tests, my MBTI is INTP-T.


My favorite number is 153 and my favorite color is yellow. In my free time, I like to draw, study about computers, linux systems, and strange programming languages; occassionally play with hardware, and work on my website. I'd like to specialize in making human-centered robotics, as I find technology a beautiful art medium.


Though I rarely play video games nowadays, I'm especially a big fan of the games Mirror's Edge and Portal 2. Both these games have played a huge part in developing my sense of self.


I stumbled across Neocities back in my freshman year of high school. During animation class, my friend [Name removed for privacy] came up to my desk and introduced me to personal websites. I signed up for an account right on the spot and immediately started making my website.
